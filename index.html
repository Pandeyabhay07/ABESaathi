About ABESEC

ABES Engineering College is one of the top engineering colleges in Uttar Pradesh. The college has received AICTE approval and is associated with AKTU, Lucknow (formerly known as Uttar Pradesh Technical University). ABES Engineering college offers various UG and PG courses such as B.Tech, MBA, MCA, and M.Tech. ABES Engineering College admission to all the courses is based on entrance and merit. Candidates not having any valid entrance exam score can also avail admission through the Management quota.
B.Tech is the flagship course at ABES Engineering college with 7 specializations. The selection is done on the basis of JEE Main score followed by UPTAC counselling. The College also offers lateral entry admission to the BTech program. Whereas admission to M.Tech is based on GATE scores and MBA and MCA admissions are based on CUET PG.
The institute has witnessed 90% placements with the highest package being offered stood at INR 47.2 LPA during placement drive of 2022. Top recruiters included Amazon, TCS, Wipro, Infosys, Mphasis, Cognizant, Accenture, etc.
